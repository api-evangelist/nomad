This guide explores how Vault acts as the control plane that generates and ingests trusted identities and maps them to policies, secrets, and certificates. It covers SPIFFE's role in portable identity representation, SPIRE's runtime verification, and four practical architectural patterns including zero-trust mTLS, JWT-SVID authentication, SPIRE integration, and Vault as upstream authority.
