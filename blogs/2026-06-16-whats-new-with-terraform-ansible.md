HashiCorp highlights three developments: the Terraform Ansible collection 2.0 powered by pyTFE for API-first workflow management, pyTFE 1.0 reaching general availability as the official Python SDK for HCP Terraform, and an experimental dynamic inventory plugin that auto-discovers Terraform-managed infrastructure. HCP Terraform also introduces a dedicated actions experience for better visibility and management of operational workflows.
