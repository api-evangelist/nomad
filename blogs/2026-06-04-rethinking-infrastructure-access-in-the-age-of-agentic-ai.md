Traditional identity and access management models fall short for AI agents, which operate with unpredictable access patterns unlike human users and may autonomously execute actions that corrupt data, trigger outages, or expose sensitive information. HashiCorp Boundary addresses these challenges by providing unique identities for agents, just-in-time credentials injected at session time rather than stored, dynamic access control, and comprehensive audit trails with session recording.
